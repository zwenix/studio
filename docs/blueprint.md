# **App Name**: EduAI Companion

## Core Features:

- AI-Powered Content Generation: Generates CAPS-compliant lesson plans, exercises, assessments, class planners, and educational posters for Grades R-12, adaptable to different learning preferences, including a tool that can adapt lessons or assignments based on insights.
- Content Upload & Memo/Rubric Generation: Allows teachers to upload custom content (tasks, assessments) with AI automatically generating memos and rubrics if not provided, ensuring alignment with CAPS standards.
- Student Assignment & Differentiation: Enables teachers to assign generated or uploaded content to individual students, groups, or the entire class, supporting differentiated instruction based on learner needs, stored in Firestore.
- Learner Performance Tracking: Collects learner academic data and generates personalized learning development plans for each child, catering to their preferred learning styles.
- Parent Portal: Provides parents with access to their child's grades and teacher feedback to foster better communication and involvement.
- Analytics Dashboard: Offers class-wide performance trends and insights to inform instructional strategies and interventions.
- Integrated OCR & Handwriting Recognition: Integrates advanced OCR to extract text from images and convert handwriting to digital text.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep sky blue (#3399FF) to evoke trust, clarity, and a sense of openness.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E5F2FF), providing a clean and unobtrusive backdrop.
- Accent color: Lime green (#B3FF33), offering a lively, optimistic contrast for key actions and elements.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' sans-serif font for titles, taglines, and navigational elements, imparting a modern, slightly technical feel.
- Body font: 'Inter' sans-serif font; clean, readable font, suited for body text and larger blocks of content.
- Use simple, clear icons with a line-art style to represent different content types and actions. Icons should maintain a consistent visual weight.
- Employ a clean, card-based layout for content, making it easy to scan and interact with different elements. Ensure ample spacing.
